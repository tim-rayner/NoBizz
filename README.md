## NoBizz

NoBizz uses cutting edge NBS technology to remove hours from your day skimming through news articles and media outlets just to figure out whats going on in your country...

### What Is NoBizz?

An AI powered google chrome extension; designed to sum up news articles into simple sentances - resolving headlines into no-bs answers to your questions.
